Welcome to The Oaks

Who are we?

The Oaks Childcare Clubs CIC is a not for profit organisation (a community interest company) providing a fun, safe place for children of different ages to play, relax and spend time before and after the school day.

The Oaks Club was started over 15 years ago and has evolved from one club run by volunteer parents to a community interest company running 6 clubs in 3 locations with over 250 children being looked after by a friend, professional and caring team of around 30 staff.

Our Clubs are based in Burghfield and Mortimer. Do get in touch if you are looking for wrap around care at your school, or if you are interested in working for us

If your child has a place at Burghfield St Mary’s Primary School, Mortimer St John’s Infant School or Mortimer St Mary’s Primary School, you can register for a place and/or contact us for more information.
