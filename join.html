Our breakfast and after school clubs are currently closed.

Work with us

We are keen to hear from you if you love working with children and enjoy being part of a committed team. In return, we provide a very competitive salary, excellent training opportunities and future career development.

Within the clubs, various term-time and flexible roles are available within breakfast club (7.15am-9.00am) or after school clubs (3.00-6.30pm) and anything between 3 and 18 hours per week are available.

If you have qualifications in Playwork, we would be interested in considering you for a Deputy Playleader or Playleader roles. We do provide training for NVQ levels 2 and 3 in Playwork.

For an informal chat and/or to receive an application form, please contact us.
