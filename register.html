covid-19

Our breakfast and after school clubs are currently closed.

Since March, we have been working closely with staff, schools, parents, considering the requirements and guidelines open to us, and we plan to re-open the clubs from September 2020.

Register

If your child attends Mortimer St Mary’s, Mortimer St John’s or Burghfield St Mary’s, you can register at any time for a place within our clubs. You can either register for regular sessions, or for one-off sessions as and when these are available. While our clubs are busy, we do have some availability and operate a waiting list where necessary. It is always working discussing with us what you need, and we will offer you a place or keep you on the waiting list for places as they become available.
We aim to send out registration forms to new school joiners in around June for a September start.
